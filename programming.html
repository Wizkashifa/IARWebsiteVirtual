Ramadan Mubarak!

As we continue under these unfortunate circumstances, we are committed to being with you every step of the way - Keeping You Connected. Integrate our Google Calendar, bookmark our fall programming homepage and download our IAR App on Android and iPhone to get automated notifications at prayers. Under the leadership of Imam Muamar Dahnoun, a number of religious rulings related to the current circumstances are posted in English and Arabic for your benefit. After reviewing our online answers, if you have additional questions please visit our click “Religious Inquiries” and we will be happy to assist.

Virtual Programming Calendar

Instagram Live Q&A

Around the Minbar

Every Fourth Tuesday of the Month at 7pm

Learn about some of the team members and work that goes into leading your beloved Islamic Association of Raleigh, and help us build a better community for you! Via Facebook Live. 

Jumuah Reminder

Friday @ 1:00 PM

Short Islamic reminder delivered from the Minbar of IAR on Fridays. Does NOT represent a virtual khutbah. Watch via YouTube. Watch previous khutbahs. 

Heart 2 Heart

Kashif Osman hosts a weekly talk show with various subject matter experts on topics such as the ongoing Yemeni Crisis or Beiruit Explosion with leading journalists, and Coronavirus update with medical experts. Via YouTube Live. 

Upcoming Imams' Class

Parting Advice

Other Programming this Fall

Get Out the Vote Initiative


As we head into a new election your masjid is proud to be part of the ONE Wake partnership to ensure our masjid has 100% voter participation. This is a non-partisan effort to ensure you have everything needed for this critical civic duty. Check if you're registered to vote or request an absentee ballot.
