Letter from the Imam

We’ve been so thrilled with the response to our “Left My Heart at the Masjid” series that we’re rolling out a bunch of new live programs as part of our virtual mosque efforts to Keep You Connected!


Between our daily Fiqh of Worship class where Imam Muamar teaches on the fiqh of salah, wudu, and other critical fiqh topics; Weight of Nation continues our popular Saturday morning class and Imam AbuTaleb presents topics from the life of Prophet Ibrahim AS and how we can apply these lessons into our lives; Sheikh Badawy's daily Quran Recitation - there's an offering for everyone and make sure to tune into our Jumuah Reminders on Friday!


Scroll down to find more about each of these programs as well as our other services, both during this time when the building is open.


The Islamic Association of Raleigh, in cooperation with the Triangle Imams Council and eleven other area masaajid, has suspended all daily prayers, Jumu'ah (Friday) prayers, and all other activities until further notice due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic. This difficult decision reflects the extraordinary circumstances of this pandemic and extensive consultation with scholars and experts.

May Allah protect protect our community and lift this affliction from all humanity.

— Your brother, Imam Mohamed AbuTaleb
